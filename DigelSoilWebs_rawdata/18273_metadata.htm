General *
* Title forest soil food web metaweb of 48 plots
* Abstract We assembled 48 forest soil food webs. We used stable isotope analyses, fatty acid analyses, mollecular gut contend analyses, body mass ranges and literature data to establish the feeding interactions. Binary food web structures can be used to calculate food web properties to describe the species interactions. The food web properties can be used to compare the soil food webs to other ecosystems. These are the first soil food webs, which were assembled using a combination of molecular methods to asses the feeding interactions. Samples were taken in 2008 and 2011.
